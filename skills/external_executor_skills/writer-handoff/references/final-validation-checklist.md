# Final Handoff Validation Checklist

- supported schema major and required envelopes;
- one pinned snapshot fingerprint;
- evidence package identity resolved;
- method package linked to code/config;
- framework figure linked to realized modules and `must_not_show`;
- result figures/tables linked to runs, configs, logs, metrics, and plot/source data;
- formal candidates have complete provenance or are downgraded;
- claim candidates have support, counterevidence, ceiling, and pending audit status;
- upstream risks, limitations, failures, approximations, replacements, and blockers propagated;
- T7 index points to existing, allowed artifacts;
- no final/audited/T8 authority fields;
- no sibling result-pack section modified;
- gate and report status consistent;
- root final validation still required.
